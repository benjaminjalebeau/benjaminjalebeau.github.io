Target Audience

The website is directed to responsible dog owners of all ages, incomes and demographics. The main motivation for someone to visit this site is to discover the weather conditions for their wanted location, and how it could effect their pet.

Personas

  • Student/Partime Worker, with dog:
    • Single
    • Busy Schedule: Works Nights, attends school in the morning.
    • Looking to plan a relaxing weekend with their pet.
    • Looking to meet new people with minimal effort.
  • Mother of three, and a dog.
    • Married
    • Stays at home during the day, attending online school.
    • Looking to schedule a camping trip during a time with good weather, and at a place that allows dogs.

Scenarios

  • When is the next friday with warm weather?

    Website gives a weather forecast for the next month.

  • Would my dog be okay if he was outside all day today?

    Website displays current day's weather. Website displays text offering advice for owner based off of the day's weather.

  • Where is the nearest dog park, and when is the next good weather day?

    Website offers list of Dog parks, and their hours. Website offers the week's weather forecast.

  • Is there any groups planning on meeting up for a hike with their dogs?

    Website displays calender with any scheduled activity. Asks user if they want to publish a scheduled activity on the site.

  • What things could I do with my dog outside today?

    Gives user the day's forecast. Suggest dog related activities based off of the weather.
