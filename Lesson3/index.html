Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to provide weather information to dog owners. This site will give the weather forcast, suggest dog related activities to the user depending on the weather, explain general safety information for dog's in weather and assist users in setting up dog/owner meetups on clear and sunny days. The goal for this website is to provide a convinient service to make plans for your dog, while also building a community of owners who interact and plan with one another.
