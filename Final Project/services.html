Services

OUR RIG

Our rig is a track-mounted CME 55. A proven, versatile rig that goes anywhere. Our CME is equipped to drill any method needed to get to the required depth. When access is difficult, whether its steep terrain, a soft approach or tight inaccessible areas, our rig can get there. It’s fast, too. Traveling from boring-to-boring won’t use up precious daylight.

HOLLOW STEM AUGER

AUGERS! We got augers! Hollow stem auger drilling is an efficient and economical method to advance the boring and facilitate sample collection. We have SPT samplers and Shelby tubes on the rig and ready to use. Our rig is equipped with a calibrated auto-hammer for reliable and consistent blow counts.

ODEX

Sometimes augers just can’t cut the mustard. No worries, we have Air/Mud Rotary and Down-Hole Hammer techniques and tooling to advance the boring when conditions get choppy.

CORING

When coring is needed, we’re here for you. Whether air or mud coring is appropriate, we have the experience and tooling to collect the information you need. We can provide core boxes and any other materials/equipment you may need to collect and store your core samples.
