Preston's Boulder Creek takes First Prize in Idaho City Chili Cook-off

IDAHO CITY, Idaho (CBS2) — More than one thousand people attended Saturday’s annual Chili Cook-Off in Idaho City. Snow, rain, sleet and even sun kept a crowd of more than 1,000 on their toes as they walked from vendor to vendor, sampling chili. Sixteen vendors were scattered throughout downtown Idaho City for folks to sample their creations and await the title of “Best Chili” from the judges. Chili was judged on color, consistency, taste/flavor and after-taste among other factors that taunt the taste buds. Read this article and more at Idahonews.com
