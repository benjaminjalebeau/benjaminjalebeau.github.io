Rule of Thirds

Gibson.com

As you can see, their guitars are placed on the top third intersections of the rule of thirds grid. The guitars are the main focus, and are placed accordingly.

PARC: Contrast

CallawayGolf.com

With a great use of contrast, Callaway puts their bright silver and green driver amidst a dark background. The logo, and navigation uses a great black on white contrast to stand out after the product.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple.com

Apple's website has plenty of whitespace all around their high focus points. You can see this in the header and main sections, as blank space surrounds the logo and phone.
